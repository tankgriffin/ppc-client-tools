FAQs

How long are sessions and what happens?

Sessions are typically 45 minutes of therapy plus time for parent feedback and planning. You’ll receive practical strategies for home.

Do you accept NDIS and private health?

Yes. We accept NDIS (self and plan‑managed), private health, Medicare and private pay.

Clinic or mobile—what’s right for us?

Clinic sessions offer specialised equipment. Mobile OT supports routines in real‑life environments. We’ll recommend the best mix.
